About

Hi! My name is Pedro. I'm a programmer currently living in Brazil.

I opened this blog in 2016 and started writing things about software engineering in it. I have always been a compiled language developer but my main progress at the time I opened it was of working with shells and their automation capabilities.

Along the way, I've programmed a number of ideas on my own. I plan to release those progressively. I have since then started crystallizing some of those concepts into increasingly formal writeups and simple implementations.

Here, you'll find the results of my work, of which I hope there is a mix of concept and utility.

Professionally, I am always looking for opportunities — areas of expertise include significant compiled code software engineering and data engineering projects in the cloud.

You can find me at psobota at pedro.sobota.nom.br .
